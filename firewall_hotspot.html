Highlights of Our Firewall and Wi-Fi Hotspot Services

Category-Based Filtering (Blocking/Whitelisting) is essential for various environments. In Schools and Educational Institutions, it allows the blocking of inappropriate content, such as adult websites, social media, and gaming sites, while whitelisting educational resources and approved websites to promote focused learning. This approach ensures compliance with regulations like the CIPA (Children’s Internet Protection Act). For Businesses and Corporates, it restricts access to non-work-related sites, including entertainment, shopping, and gambling, while permitting access to essential business tools and websites. In Public Spaces, it blocks malicious or harmful content to protect users and allows for customized filters based on the audience, such as family-friendly settings in malls.

Intrusion Detection and Prevention System (IDPS) plays a crucial role in network security. It offers Real-Time Threat Detection to identify and block malicious activities, including DDoS attacks, malware, and unauthorized access attempts. The system features Automated Response capabilities that automatically mitigate threats by blocking suspicious IP addresses or traffic patterns. With Comprehensive Monitoring, it continuously observes network traffic for anomalies and potential breaches, while Custom Alerts provide instant notifications for critical security events.

Seamless Wi-Fi Access enhances user experience by providing hassle-free internet connectivity through multiple authentication options, such as social logins (Facebook, Google), SMS-based OTP (One-Time Password), and voucher codes for time-limited access. It also supports captive portals that redirect users to a splash page upon connection.

User Management and Analytics enable tracking of user activity, session duration, and data usage. This feature provides valuable insights into customer behavior and preferences through detailed analytics, allowing for the setting of usage limits or time restrictions to promote fair usage policies.

Revolutionizing Food Ordering

Innovative QR and URL-Based System for Seamless Ordering

In today’s fast-paced world, convenience and efficiency are essential to delivering exceptional customer experiences. At Iberry WiFi Corporation, we’ve developed an innovative QR and URL-based food ordering system that transforms the way customers order food—whether they’re in hotel rooms, at restaurant tables, or in nearby outdoor areas.

Our system eliminates the need for traditional menus, wait staff intervention, and long waiting times. With just a scan or a click, customers can browse menus, place orders, and make payments effortlessly. For businesses, this means streamlined operations, reduced costs, and happier customers.

How It Works For You?

Generate QR Codes & URLs: We create unique QR codes and URLs for your business, linked to your customized digital menu and ordering platform.

Display QR Codes: Place QR codes in hotel rooms, on restaurant tables, or in outdoor areas like parks and food courts.

Customers Scan or Click: Customers scan the QR code with their smartphone or click the URL to access your menu and ordering system.

Place Orders & Pay: Customers browse the menu, select items, and place orders directly through the platform. Integrated payment options make checkout quick and easy.

Order Fulfillment: Orders are sent directly to your kitchen or staff for preparation and delivery.

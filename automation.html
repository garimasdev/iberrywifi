Enhance Efficiency and Security with Office Automation Technologies

Automated Lighting and Climate Control Systems: These systems utilize sensors and smart technology to adjust lighting and temperature based on occupancy and time of day, promoting energy efficiency and creating a comfortable work environment.

Voice-Activated Assistants: Devices like Amazon Echo or Google Home can be integrated into office settings to control various functions such as scheduling meetings, setting reminders, or managing smart devices through voice commands.

Automated Visitor Management Systems: These systems streamline the process of registering and monitoring visitors within office premises. Visitors can sign in using digital kiosks, and the system can notify hosts automatically, enhancing security and efficiency.

Smart Surveillance Systems: Advanced security cameras equipped with motion detection and facial recognition can monitor office spaces in real-time, sending alerts for any unusual activities and allowing for remote access to surveillance feeds.

Automated Inventory Management: Utilizing RFID tags and barcode systems, businesses can automatically track inventory levels, manage stock, and generate orders when supplies run low, reducing manual effort and minimizing errors.

Why Choose Our Automation Solutions?

The Power of Combining BLE + LoRaWAN The Power of Combining BLE + LoRaWAN creates a robust 360° automation ecosystem. BLE excels in indoor precision, while LoRaWAN effectively manages broader outdoor areas. This synergy enables hybrid use cases, such as tracking tools in a warehouse, smart lighting with BLE and monitoring delivery trucks, for garden irrigation in a smart home with LoRaWAN.

Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) Beacons At Iberry Wifi Corporation, our Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) beacons serve as your proximity-powered assistant, enabling smart lighting and climate control, indoor navigation, asset tracking, and personalized experiences that enhance user interaction. For instance, in an office setting, beacons can automatically adjust lighting and AV systems when a conference room is occupied, providing a seamless and efficient environment.

LoRaWAN (Long Range Wide Area Network) acts as the backbone of long-range automation, making it ideal for outdoor security, energy management, environmental monitoring, and smart agriculture. An example use case includes LoRaWAN sensors detecting a water leak in a smart home and instantly alerting your phone, even from miles away. This technology ensures effective monitoring and management across vast areas, providing peace of mind and convenience.

Additionally, this reduces infrastructure costs by applying the right technology for specific tasks and offers future-proof flexibility, allowing seamless integration with existing systems like Alexa, Google Home, and various enterprise IoT platforms.

QR-Based Intercom System

Revolutionizing Communication for Modern Businesses

Introduction

At Iberry WiFi Corporation, we're revolutionizing the way businesses communicate with their clients. Our QR-based intercom system eliminates the need for toll-free numbers or complex IVR setups.

With just a simple QR code or URL, your clients can connect directly to your intercom or IVR system.

How It Works

1

Generate Your QR Code or URL

We create a unique QR code and URL for your business, customized with your preferred IVR options.

2

Share with Clients

Send the QR code or URL to your clients via SMS, WhatsApp, email, or print materials.

3

Scan & Connect

Clients scan the QR code or click the link to access a user-friendly interface.

4

Seamless Communication

The system connects them to the right department without toll-free numbers.

Key Features

  • No Toll-Free Numbers Required: Save costs by eliminating expensive toll-free numbers.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Simple, intuitive menu for a smooth experience.
  • Customizable IVR Options: Tailor options to match your business needs.

FAQs

Do clients need to download an app to use this system?

No! Our QR-based intercom system works directly through a web interface—no apps or downloads required.

Can I customize the IVR options?

Yes, you can fully customize the options to match your business needs (e.g., sales, support, extensions).

Is this system compatible with all devices?

Absolutely! The system works on any smartphone or device with a camera and internet connection.

How do I share the QR code or URL with clients?

You can share it via SMS, WhatsApp, email, or even print it on physical materials like brochures or posters.

This solution eliminates the need for toll-free numbers and complex IVR setups, making communication more efficient and accessible for both businesses and customers.

How It Works

  • 1. Generate Your QR Code or URL: We create a unique QR code and URL for your business, customized with your preferred IVR options (e.g., sales, support, extensions).
  • 2. Share with Clients: Send the QR code or URL via SMS, WhatsApp, email, or print it on brochures, menus, or posters.
  • 3. Scan & Connect: Clients simply scan the QR code or click the link to access a user-friendly interface. They can select the desired option or dial an extension directly.
  • 4. Seamless Communication: The system connects them to the right department or person without the need for toll-free numbers or additional apps.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions About Our CCTV Solutions

What is the difference between analog and IP CCTV cameras?
Analog cameras transmit video signals to a DVR, while IP cameras send digital video over a network, offering higher resolution and advanced features.
Can I access my CCTV footage remotely?
Yes, our systems allow you to access live and recorded footage from your smartphone, tablet, or computer.
Do you offer night vision cameras?
Absolutely! Our outdoor CCTV cameras come equipped with night vision for 24/7 surveillance.
How long is the footage stored?
Storage duration depends on your system configuration. We offer options like local storage (DVR/NVR) and cloud storage for extended retention.

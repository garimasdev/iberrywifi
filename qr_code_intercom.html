How It Works

Generate Your QR Code or URL

We create a unique QR code and URL for your business, customized with your preferred IVR options.

Share with Clients

Send the QR code or URL to your clients via SMS, WhatsApp, email, or even print it on brochures, menus, or posters.

Scan & Connect

Clients simply scan the QR code or click the link to access a user-friendly interface. They can select the desired option (e.g., sales, support) or dial an extension directly.

Seamless Communication

The system connects them to the right department or person without the need for toll-free numbers or additional apps.
